Nebraska, USA

Nebraska is the 37th state, it was admitted into the union on March 1, 1867. It is landlocked state in th Midwest ans is borderd by South Dakota to the North, Kansas to the South, Wyoming and Colorado to the west, Missouri and Iowa to the east. It is the 16th largest state by land area with a population over 2 million. It is primarily and agriculture and cattle ranch state. It is part of tornado alley where it has severe storms and on average there are 30=50 tornados a year. Some cool facts about Nebraska is it is the birthplace of Kool-aid, and has one of the highest rated zoo's in the USA in the city of Omaha, it also know for Chimney Rock and Carhenge. It is also know for the Runza which dates back to the Germanic settlers.
